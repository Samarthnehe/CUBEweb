Riviera Cube Open

Riviera Cube Open is an intra-VIT cubing competetion, where students from VIT and schools in vicinity participate to win various cubing TITLES. We organise events like 3x3,2x2,4x4,5x5,Pyraminx,Megaminx and 3x3 One Handed. Also here we have a workshop on the 1st day to teach students how to CUBE!!
